Empowering Through Action

Our mission is to uplift differently-abled individuals through training, social initiatives, and community welfare programs across Maharashtra.

Programs & Social Impact

Zunj Divyang Sanstha Ashram Training Center has been active since 2014 in Pimpri-Chinchwad, providing opportunities and hope to differently-abled individuals through education, training, and empowerment programs.

Key Initiatives

  • Residential facility for more than 20 differently-abled students.
  • Vocational courses: mobile repairing, tailoring, data entry, and beauty parlour operations.
  • 72 free wedding ceremonies organized for differently-abled couples with complete household support.
  • Distribution of UDID (Unique Disability ID) cards to over 300 beneficiaries.
  • Provision of mobility aids: wheelchairs, crutches, calipers, and tricycles.
  • Housing support with Pimpri-Chinchwad MCC — 350 beneficiaries received ₹1,00,000 grants.
  • COVID-19 relief drives: awareness campaigns, mask and sanitizer distribution, and tailoring kits for 100+ beneficiaries.
  • Online training partnership with GTT Foundation for mobile repair courses (350 students).
  • Monthly stipend support (₹5,000) for trainees via NHFDC partnership.

Impact

Over 2000 students trained, thousands empowered to start independent businesses, and continuous outreach supporting health, housing, and social dignity.
