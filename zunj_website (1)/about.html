About the Organization

Founded in 2014, Zunj Divyang Sanstha Ashram Training Center is dedicated to empowering differently-abled individuals through vocational education, residential training, and social programs across Maharashtra. The center provides a supportive residential environment for over 20 students and runs multiple initiatives to secure rights and livelihoods for the disabled community.

Book

Our Mission & Vision

To create an inclusive environment where differently-abled individuals can live with dignity, independence, and opportunity. Through education, skill-building, and community engagement, we strive to break barriers and foster self-reliance.

Vision: A society where every person, regardless of ability, has equal access to growth, empowerment, and respect.

Message from the Founder

“Our goal is simple — to stand beside those who are often left behind, to help them discover their strength and purpose. Disability should never define one’s potential. Together, we can create opportunities that change lives.”

— Raju Kadappa Hirave
